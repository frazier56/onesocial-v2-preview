Data Deletion Instructions

OneSocial (onesocial.ai) lets you delete your account and any data we have collected from connected social platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) at any time.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Sign in at https://onesocial.ai.
  2. Go to Settings → Connected Apps and click Disconnect next to any social platform. This removes that platform's tokens and any media we imported from it.
  3. To delete your entire OneSocial account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Option 2 — Email request

Send an email to privacy@onesocial.ai from the email address associated with your OneSocial account. Include:

  • Your account email
  • The platform(s) you want disconnected (e.g. Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn), or "delete entire account"

We will process the request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • OAuth tokens for the disconnected platform
  • Any imported posts, media, and metadata sourced from that platform
  • For full account deletion: profile, messages, reviews, endorsements, and all associated records
